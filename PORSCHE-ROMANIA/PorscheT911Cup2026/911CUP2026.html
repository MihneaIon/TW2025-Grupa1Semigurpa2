The Porsche 911 Cup

PURE ENOUGH TO RUIN EVERYTHING ELSE YOU'LL EVER DRIVE.

Precision at the limit

The new 911 Cup. Built for those who demand precision at the limit. Every line of its bodywork is shaped by purpose – from the three-piece front lip to the swan-neck rear wing. Louvres in the fenders breathe out air, while the underbody and turning vanes carve the air with surgical intent. This isn’t just a race car. It’s a scalpel for the track.

Technical Specs

Performance in numbers

Engine Water-cooled six-cylinder boxer
Displacement 3,996 cm3
Performance about 382 kW (520 hp)
Gears Porsche six-speed sequential dog-type transmission
Weight about 1288 kg
Drive line Rear-wheel drive

Thomas Laudenbach, Vice President Porsche Motorsport

Naturally Aspirated. Unfiltered. Uncompromising.

A 4.0-liter flat-six that screams to 9,000 rpm, now with 520 horsepower. No turbos. No excuses. Just throttle, revs, and the kind of mechanical honesty that rewards the brave. With individual throttle bodies and longer valve lift, it's built to breathe – and to dominate.

Maximum Control When It Counts

Wider discs, better cooling, and a brake system that's been reengineered for durability. The braking system is delivering all the feedback you'll need – because confidence under pressure is everything. Greater friction surface and more ventilation means you can go deeper, lap after lap.

Race-Ready Focus

Inside, it's all business. No distractions. No compromise. A new multifunction steering wheel puts more control at your fingertips. Dedicated switches, more clarity. A new display touch panel unlocks deep settings without a laptop – because when seconds count, simplicity wins.
